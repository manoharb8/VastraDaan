Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a donation pickup?

After logging in, go to your dashboard and click "Donate Now". Fill in the details about the items you wish to donate, and our team will schedule a pickup at your registered address.

What items can I donate?

We accept all kinds of new or gently used clothing in fair, good, or new condition. This includes shirts, pants, dresses, and traditional wear. We do not accept undergarments or socks.

How can I track my donation?

You can track the status of your most recent donation by clicking the "Track" link in the navigation menu after you have logged in.
